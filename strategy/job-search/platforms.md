## Application Rules
1. Apply to max 5 companies/day (quality over quantity)
2. Always customize resume variant for the role
3. Track every application in strategy/job-search/tracker.md
4. Follow up after 1 week if no response
5. Never apply with ❌ UNVERIFIED claims on resume
