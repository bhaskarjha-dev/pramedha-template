# Outreach — Networking & Referral Playbook

> **Purpose:** Templates for cold outreach, warm intros, and referral requests.

---

## Cold LinkedIn Message Template

> Hi [Name], I came across your work at [Company] and was impressed by [specific detail].
> I'm a [your role] specializing in [your domain], and I'm exploring opportunities in [area].
> Would you be open to a brief chat about your experience at [Company]?

## Referral Request Template

> Hi [Name], I hope you're doing well! I noticed [Company] has an opening for [Role]
> that aligns closely with my experience in [domain]. I've [specific achievement].
> Would you be comfortable referring me? I've attached my resume for your reference.

## Follow-up Template (1 week after application)

> Hi [Name], I wanted to follow up on my application for [Role] submitted on [date].
> I'm particularly excited about [specific aspect of role/company].
> Please let me know if there's any additional information I can provide.
