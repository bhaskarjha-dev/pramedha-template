# Application Tracker

> **Purpose:** Track every application from submission to outcome.
> Update this at least weekly during active job search.

---

## Pipeline Stages

| Stage | Emoji | Definition | Expected Timeline |
|-------|:-----:|-----------|:-----------------:|
| Identified | 🔍 | Found the listing, haven't applied | — |
| Applied | 📤 | Application submitted | Day 0 |
| Screen | 📞 | Recruiter/HR call | Week 1-2 |
| Technical | 💻 | Technical interview (coding/system design) | Week 2-4 |
| Behavioral | 🤝 | Culture/behavioral/hiring manager round | Week 3-5 |
| Offer | 📋 | Offer received | Week 4-6 |
| Accepted | ✅ | Offer accepted | — |
| Rejected | ❌ | At any stage | — |
| Ghosted | ⏸️ | No response after 2+ weeks | — |
| Withdrawn | 🚫 | You withdrew from process | — |

---

## Follow-up Rules

| Trigger | Action | Template |
|---------|--------|----------|
| After application (Day 7) | Follow up via email or LinkedIn | See `job-search/outreach.md` |
| After interview (within 24h) | Thank-you email to each interviewer | Personalized, reference specific discussion point |
| After ghosted (Day 14) | One follow-up, then move on | "Checking in on the status of my application..." |
| After rejection | Thank them, ask for feedback | "I appreciate the update. If possible, I'd value any feedback..." |
