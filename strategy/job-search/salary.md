## Negotiation Scripts

### When asked "What's your current salary?"
> "I'd prefer to focus on the value I bring to this role. Based on my research,
> the market range for this position is [range]. I'm looking for [your target]."

### When given a lowball offer
> "Thank you for the offer. I'm very excited about the role. Based on my
> [experience/skills/market research], I was expecting something in the range of
> [your target]. Is there flexibility to discuss this?"

### When asked for expected salary
> "Based on my research and the scope of this role, I'm targeting [range].
> I'm open to discussing the full compensation package including [equity/bonus/benefits]."
