# Content Plan — Thought Leadership & Publishing

> **Purpose:** Build public credibility through consistent content.
> Your content is your "proof of thought" — it shows you think deeply about your domain.

---

## Content Pillars (Choose 3)

> A "pillar" is a topic you become known for. Pick based on:
> - What you know deeply (expertise)
> - What people need to learn (demand)
> - What you enjoy discussing (sustainability)

1. **Pillar:** (e.g., "AI Engineering in Production")
2. **Pillar:** (e.g., "Career Growth for Mid-Level Engineers")
3. **Pillar:** (e.g., "Open Source & Developer Tools")

## Publishing Schedule

| Platform | Frequency | Content Type | Pillar |
|----------|:---------:|-------------|:------:|
| LinkedIn | 2-3x/week | Insights, stories, learnings | 1, 2 |
| Blog/Medium | Biweekly | Deep-dive articles, tutorials | 1, 3 |
| GitHub | Ongoing | Projects, contributions, READMEs | 3 |
| Twitter/X | Daily | Quick thoughts, thread summaries | 1, 2 |

---

## Content Repurposing Chain

> Write once, publish everywhere:

```
Blog post (long-form)
  → LinkedIn article (adapted)
  → Twitter/X thread (key points)
  → LinkedIn carousel (visual summary)
  → Newsletter snippet
```

## Content Quality Checklist

Before publishing any piece:
- [ ] Does it teach something actionable?
- [ ] Is there a specific example or story?
- [ ] Would I share this if someone else wrote it?
- [ ] Does it end with a question or call-to-action?
- [ ] Is it under the platform's ideal length?
