## The "Hell Yes" Test

> A role should pass at least 3 of these:

- [ ] I'd be excited to tell friends about this job
- [ ] I'd learn skills that matter in 5 years
- [ ] The compensation meets my financial goals
- [ ] The work-life balance supports my non-negotiables
- [ ] I respect the people I'd work with
- [ ] The company's mission resonates with me
