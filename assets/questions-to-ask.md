# Questions To Ask — Interview Preparation

> **Purpose:** Prepared questions to ask interviewers.
> Shows engagement and helps you evaluate the company.

---

## About The Role
- What does a typical day/week look like in this role?
- What are the biggest challenges the team is facing right now?
- How is success measured for this position?
- What does the onboarding process look like?

## About The Team
- How is the team structured?
- What's the team's working style? (async, meetings, pair programming)
- How does the team handle disagreements on technical decisions?

## About Growth
- What does career progression look like for this role?
- How does the company support learning and development?
- Are there opportunities to work across teams?

## About The Company
- What's the company's biggest priority for the next year?
- How does this team contribute to the company's core mission?
- What's the company's approach to remote/hybrid work?

## Red Flag Detectors
- Why is this position open? (New role vs backfill)
- What's the typical tenure of people in this role?
- Can you describe the work-life balance expectations?
