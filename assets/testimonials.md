# Testimonials — Social Proof & Endorsements

> **Purpose:** Collect quotes from people who can vouch for your work.
> Use these in: portfolio, LinkedIn featured section, cover letters, personal website.
>
> These are NOT references (see `identity/references.yaml`).
> These are public-facing endorsements you have permission to share.

---

## How To Collect

1. After a successful project: "Would you mind sharing a quick quote about our work together?"
2. From LinkedIn recommendations: Copy relevant excerpts (with permission)
3. From Slack/email praise: Screenshot or copy the message (ask if you can use it)
4. From performance reviews: Extract positive quotes (anonymize if needed)
