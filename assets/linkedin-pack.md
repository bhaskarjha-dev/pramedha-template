# LinkedIn Content Pack

> **Purpose:** Optimized content for your LinkedIn profile sections.
> LinkedIn is the #1 professional discovery platform. Treat your profile as a product page.

---

## Profile Optimization Checklist

- [ ] **Photo:** Professional headshot, face fills 60% of frame, solid/simple background
- [ ] **Banner:** Custom image showing your domain (tech stack, project, brand visual)
- [ ] **Custom URL:** linkedin.com/in/yourname (Settings → Edit public profile URL)
- [ ] **Headline:** Uses target role keywords, not just current title (max 220 chars)
- [ ] **About section:** Story-driven, not resume copy-paste (see below)
- [ ] **Experience:** Every entry has 2-3 bullet points with metrics
- [ ] **Skills:** Top 3 are your target role skills, not generic ones
- [ ] **Recommendations:** At least 3 (manager + peer + stakeholder)
- [ ] **Featured:** Best project, published article, or conference talk pinned
- [ ] **Activity:** Posted or engaged in last 30 days

---

## Headline

> Max 220 chars. Formula: **[Role] | [Specialization] | [Differentiator]**
>
> Examples:
> - "Senior Backend Engineer | Distributed Systems & GenAI | Building at Scale"
> - "Product Manager | B2B SaaS | Ex-Google, Ex-Flipkart"

## About Section

> 2-3 paragraphs. Structure:
> - **Paragraph 1:** Who you are + what you do (hook)
> - **Paragraph 2:** Your journey + key achievements (credibility)
> - **Paragraph 3:** What you're looking for / how to reach you (CTA)
>
> End with a keyword block for searchability.

## Experience Section Writing Guide

> Write each entry like a mini resume bullet. NOT "Responsible for managing..."
>
> Formula: **"[Action verb] [what you did] → [measurable impact]"**
>
> Examples:
> - ✅ "Designed and shipped real-time notification system serving 2M users"
> - ❌ "Responsible for the notification system"

---

## Recommendations Strategy

> Ask people who can speak to specific strengths.

| Who To Ask | Relationship | What They'll Speak To | Status |
|-----------|-------------|----------------------|:------:|
| Direct manager | Supervised your work | Work quality, reliability | |
| Peer / colleague | Worked alongside you | Collaboration, technical skill | |
| Client / stakeholder | Received your output | Impact, communication | |

> **Template for asking:**
> "Hi [Name], I'm updating my LinkedIn profile and your perspective on
> [specific project/skill] would mean a lot. Would you be comfortable
> writing a brief recommendation? Happy to reciprocate!"
