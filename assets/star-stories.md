# STAR Stories — Behavioral Interview Preparation

> **Purpose:** Pre-written behavioral interview stories using the STAR framework.
> Each story demonstrates a core competency with a concrete example from YOUR career.
>
> STAR = Situation, Task, Action, Result
>
> **How To Use:**
> 1. Before an interview, identify which competencies the role tests
> 2. Pick 2-3 stories that demonstrate those competencies
> 3. Practice telling each story in under 2 minutes
> 4. Quantify results wherever possible

---

## Story 1: Leadership & Initiative

**Competency:** Leading without authority, taking ownership, driving outcomes

**Situation:**
> What was the context? What challenge did your team face?

**Task:**
> What was your specific responsibility? Why did YOU step up?

**Action:**
> What did YOU do? (Use "I", not "we"). Be specific about decisions made.

**Result:**
> What was the measurable outcome? What changed because of your action?

**Variations:**
- For "initiative" focus: emphasize why you acted without being asked
- For "ownership" focus: emphasize end-to-end responsibility
