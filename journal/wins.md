# Wins — Achievement Log

> **Purpose:** Chronological record of career achievements.
> Append-only — never delete entries. Date each win.
>
> This is your "brag document." Update it immediately after each win,
> not weeks later when details are fuzzy.
>
> Cross-references:
> - Major wins → consider adding to `identity/experience.yaml` bullets
> - Skills demonstrated → update `identity/skills.yaml` evidence
> - Evidence created → log in `identity/claims-and-evidence.md`

---

## Template

```
### YYYY-MM-DD — [Title]
**Impact:** Minor / Moderate / Major
**Context:** What was the situation?
**Achievement:** What did you accomplish?
**Evidence:** How can you prove it? (link, screenshot, metric)
**Skills demonstrated:** [list]
**Used in:** (link to experience.yaml bullet or master.yaml key, if applicable)
```
